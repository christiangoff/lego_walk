Walk while you build.

StudStep is a private fitness tracker built for people who love Lego and want to stay active. Log every treadmill session, link it to a set you're building, and watch your stats grow alongside your collection.

  • Track distance, speed, duration, and calories
  • Link sessions to specific Lego sets and bags
  • See your progress grow build by build

Features

Everything you need to track your Lego walking journey.

Session Logging

Record each walk with distance, speed, duration, calories, and which Lego bags you finished.

Set Gallery

Browse every set you've worked on, with images pulled from Rebrickable, piece counts, and completion status.

Per-Set Breakdown

Drill into any set to see every session, total miles walked, calories burned, and pieces built.

Stats & Charts

Visualise your activity over time — distance trends, weekly summaries, and calorie history.

Weight & Profile

Log your weight over time, track BMI, and keep your profile up to date for accurate calorie estimates.

Rebrickable Lookup

Enter a set number and auto-fill the name, piece count, theme, and cover image from Rebrickable.
